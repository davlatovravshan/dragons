About Us

In the beginning, there was a primordial being called, Gaia. Gaia created the world in 5 days from the land to its people. But the creation process killed her, before she died, Gaia created 5 dragons to help guide humanity. It was peaceful at the time until a war broke out between humanity entangling the dragons with it. Humanity lived in unity but the conflict separated them. The war only ended when a child died and in that moment, a peace treaty was signed, resulting in five dragons dividing the world and ruling their respective regions.
